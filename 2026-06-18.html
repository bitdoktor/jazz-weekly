★ Berklee-trained bebop saxophonist; RI Music Hall of Fame, DownBeat Readers Poll top-4 alto.

Greg Abate Quartet

Jazz
Scullers Jazz Club · Boston T Green · Babcock Street · 15 min 7:00 PM

Greg Abate (b. 1947, Fall River, MA) is a Berklee-trained bebop and hard-bop saxophonist who held the lead alto chair in the Ray Charles Band (1973-74) and later played with the revived Artie Shaw Orchestra. A prolific international touring artist, he has recorded extensively, including on the Candid jazz label, and was inducted into the Rhode Island Music Hall of Fame (2016).

Berklee-trained bebop saxophonist; RI Music Hall of Fame, DownBeat Readers Poll top-4 alto.
